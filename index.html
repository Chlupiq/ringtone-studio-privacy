# Privacy Policy — Ringtone Studio *Last updated: March 2025* --- ## Overview Ringtone Studio ("the App") is developed and maintained by Martin ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information. **Short version: we collect nothing. Your files never leave your device.** --- ## Information We Do Not Collect Ringtone Studio does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically: - We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal identifiers - We do not access your contacts, location, camera, or microphone - We do not track your usage or behavior within the App - We do not use analytics or crash reporting services - We do not display advertisements - We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies --- ## How the App Works The App processes audio files entirely on your device: 1. You select an MP3 file from your local Files app 2. The App trims and processes the file locally on your iPhone 3. The resulting ringtone is saved to your device 4. No audio data is uploaded to any server at any point All processing happens on-device. The App does not require an internet connection. --- ## File Access The App accesses audio files only when you explicitly select them using the system file picker. The App does not scan, index, or access any other files on your device. Saved ringtones are stored locally in the App's designated storage and are accessible via the History tab. --- ## User-Provided Content & Disclaimer Ringtone Studio is a tool that processes audio files provided solely by the user. It is safe for all ages. --- ## Changes to This Policy If we ever update this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
