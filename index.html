Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Ringtone Studio is built around one goal — getting your song onto your phone as a ringtone, as fast as possible.

Real Waveform
See the actual audio of your song — not a placeholder. Pinch to zoom in for frame-perfect trimming.
Precision Trimming
Drag the selection handles to pick any 30-second clip. The length chip updates in real time as you adjust.
Loop Preview
Play your selection on a continuous loop while you adjust handles. Hear every change instantly.
Smooth Fades
One slider controls fade in and fade out simultaneously. No technical knowledge needed.
Normalize Volume
Boost quiet recordings automatically so your ringtone sounds great on the first ring.
Ringtone History
Every saved ringtone stays in your History tab — ready to re-share or set again anytime.

How it works

From song to ringtone
in under a minute.

1
Import your MP3
Tap "Choose MP3 File" and pick any track from your Files app.
2
Trim to 30 seconds
Drag the waveform handles to select your perfect clip. Pinch to zoom for precision.
3
Preview on loop
Hit Preview and enable loop. Adjust until it sounds exactly right.
4
Save & set
Tap Trim & Save, then Share → Use as Ringtone. Done.
